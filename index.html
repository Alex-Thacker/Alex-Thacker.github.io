Projects

Land Ocean for Humans

My front end capstone. This app was designed to help people gain access to a local swimming pool. The app has two main features, sharing your pool and finding a pool. If the user has a pool, they're able to share it with the public and if they don't have a pool, they're able to find one that other users have shared. The app also features a weather forecast system through darkSky api, so you know if its a good day to swim or not.

Nutshell

Nutshell was our third group project at NSS. Mostly done in Vanilla JavaScript, Nutshell is designed to be similar to a social media page, featuring sections for tasks, articles, events, a chat room, and a friends list.
